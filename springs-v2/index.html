About Us

We are Springs Foundation, Inc — a non-profit company dedicated to spreading the Good News.

Our mission is to inspire Catholics to grow deeper in their relationship with Jesus and to apply God’s Word, so that they can live lives to the full and be a blessing to others.

Through our various publications, music and audio resources, and events, we believe we are making an impact in their lives now and for eternity.

With our goal of proclaiming the Gospel, we are one with the Church’s call to a New Evangelization. We also aim to serve the Church by providing creative and technological support through Springs Production Studios and our digital archiving services.

Springs Foundation, Inc is the corporate arm of Elim Communities. Elim Communities comprises over 170 Catholic communities in the Philippines, as well as in Hong Kong, Singapore, Japan, Canada and the United States of America.

Services

Springs serves as a media center through Springs Production Studios, with its own facilities for audio-video productions, taping facilities for radio and TV programs and commercials, plus various studios for song and dance rehearsals, dubbing, and post production.

Aside from this, Springs also has various rental venues suitable for various events such as meetings, seminars, private celebrations and conferences. The auditorium, which can accommodate 800 to 1,000 persons, has become a popular venue for theater and stage play performances, especially for schools.

Springs has established relationships with various clients. With the two top Philippine media networks just 10 minutes away, Springs is often tapped for its facilities for both commercials and program tapings.

Products & Events

Springs offers various resources to inspire today’s Catholic, such as publications, music and audio teachings.

One of its publications is the annual Living Water Bible reflection guide, which is read by tens of thousands of people every year.

Its songs — most especially its version of the Ama Namin — have found its way into many parishes.

And every year, Springs organizes events such as the Elim Youth Camp, the Inner Beauty Women’s Conference, and the Excellence Conference that bring together people from all over the Philippines and abroad.

Spring Charities

Springs gives back through Springs Charities. Its projects include providing clean drinking water for poor communities, donations of medical equipment and supplies to medical institutions, medical missions, relief and rehabilitation efforts during calamities, scholarships for training seminars and conferences.

Awards

Springs’ integrity as an institution, coupled with a passion for excellence, has reaped various awards for creativity such as original song compositions, radio productions, televisions shows, and publications.
